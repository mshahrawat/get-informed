Product

our integrated hardware and software solution

Encourages Adherence

Enable smart reminders to take your pills on time, and get alerts soon after you missed your time. See trends about your usage habits and tips on how to make your usage more effective.

Easy Setup

Simply put your phone and pill pack in our integrated pill containing smart phone case, connect the phone case with your phone's Bluetooth, download the app, and you're ready to go!

No Extra Hardware

With our smart yet discrete phone case, you don't have to carry around anything extra, making it great for travel and work. Pills can stay in the original packaging, and no extra charging necessary, just charge your phone and case as usual.

Features

Learn about the key features of our smart phone pill case.

  • Accurate

    Sensing Technology

    Our smart sensing technology knows how many pills are left in your pack so that it can remind you take a pill if you missed one.

  • Portable

    Phone Case Form Factor

    Store your pills in your phone case and never forget them at home.

  • Data-driven

    Real-time Analytics

    The phone app offers real-time usage statistics, side effects trends, and tips at your fingertips.
